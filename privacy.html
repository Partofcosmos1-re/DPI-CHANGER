Last Updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use the DPI Converter website (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We believe in privacy by design. Our service is built to be as private as possible:

  • No User Accounts: We do not require you to create an account to use our Service.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or have access to any personal information like your name, email address, or IP address.
  • Image Processing: All image processing, including changing the DPI, is done directly in your web browser on your own device. Your images are never uploaded to our servers. They remain on your computer at all times.

2. Cookies and Local Storage

  • Local Storage: We use your browser's local storage to save your preference for light or dark mode. This is for your convenience and does not contain any personal information.

Google may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your files or personal data on our servers, the risk of a data breach from our Service is eliminated. You are in full control of your files.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
